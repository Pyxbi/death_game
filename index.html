Game Rules

  • Your goal is to clear as many stages as possible to maximize your score.
  • Each stage has a set number of cards you must pick successfully.
  • Most cards contain points. Some cards are 'Booms'.
  • If you click a 'Boom', you lose one of your 4 tries but must still complete your picks for the stage.
  • If you run out of tries, the game is over and your score is halved.
  • After completing Stage 3, you will have the option to 'Give Up' and keep your current score, or continue to the 'Next Stage'.
  • Clear all stages to win!
